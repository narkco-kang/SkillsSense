## 📝 Description（200 chars）

SkillsSense understands your intent in natural language, searches across multiple sources (HuggingFace, GitHub, Awesome Lists), and delivers curated skills with AI-generated tutorials. No more "I don't know what to search for" — just describe your goal.

---

## 💬 常見問題（FAQ）

**Q: How is this different from just Googling?**
A: Google gives you lists. SkillsSense gives you answers — it understands what you're trying to achieve and generates a step-by-step tutorial with the best tools for your specific use case.

**Q: Is it free?**
A: Yes, the MVP is completely free during launch period.

**Q: What sources does it search?**
A: HuggingFace, GitHub Topics, Awesome Lists, and local curated skills — all searched in parallel for fast results.

**Q: Does it work in languages other than English?**
A: Yes! SkillsSense supports both English and Traditional Chinese at launch, with more languages coming soon.
